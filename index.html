Written by Charles Ko
Under supervision of Professor Larry Sorensen
University of Washington

This tool uses the relaxation method to solve a Laplace's equation in a rectangular region with boundary conditions. The purposes of this tool are to help science students build intuition on how the solution would look and to demonstrate the numerical method used to solve it.

Github Repo: https://github.com/charlieko/laplace-solver
